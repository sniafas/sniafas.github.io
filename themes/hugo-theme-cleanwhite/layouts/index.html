Short Bio

Stavros Niafas has a major in Informatics Engineering while he also holds two Msc's, in Data Science and in Image Synthesis & Multimedia. As a professional ML practitioner has demonstrated experience in both R&D and production settings. He helps teams to democratize research and reach faster to production production, from driving ML experiments and PoC's into mature codebases to support e2e ML pipelines and systems. He is a results oriented individual and perceives his craft as a mean to achieve a certain goal. While he keeps a high sense of ownership, he genuinely cares to promote and establish a healthy AI culture within the teams and seeks ways to translate in simple terms, complex notions to non-tech audiences. He specializes in computer vision while his research interests expand in other domains of ML, predictive analysis, timeseries forecasting and NLP. He is also actively engaged in open source contributions, MLOps and systems engineering with strong foundations in Linux environments.

In analogue world, photography is his passion and vinyl collection his secret affair.
